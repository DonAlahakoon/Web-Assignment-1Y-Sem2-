What is Global Climate Change(GCC)?

A noticeable variation in the average weather condition over a long period of time can be defined as a climate change.Such changes when considered globally, is called as Global Climate Change. The change can either be due to natural phenomenas or a result of certain activities of living beings especially humans. The result of this change gives adverse effect which endangers all the species on earth. Common result of GCC is melting of glaciers, global warming, air pollution, depletion of Ozone layer.
